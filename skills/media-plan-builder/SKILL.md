# Media Plan Builder

Creates comprehensive media plans including channel mix recommendations by objective, budget allocation by flight/phase, CPM/CPC/CPA projections by platform, reach and frequency estimation, and funnel stage mapping from awareness through conversion.

## Capabilities

- **Channel Mix Recommendations**: Platform selection based on objective and audience
- **Budget Allocation**: Phase-based budgeting with ramp-up and optimization periods
- **Cost Projections**: CPM, CPC, CPA, and ROAS estimates by platform and vertical
- **Reach & Frequency Estimation**: Audience sizing and optimal frequency caps
- **Funnel Mapping**: Awareness → Consideration → Conversion stage planning
- **Flight Planning**: Campaign phasing with learning period budgets
- **Template Generation**: Exportable media plan tables

### 3. Budget Allocation by Flight/Phase

**Standard 3-Phase Campaign Structure:**

```
Phase 1: LEARNING (Weeks 1-2) — 20% of budget
  Objective: Establish baselines, test creative, build pixel data
  Approach: Broader targeting, multiple ad variants, CBO (Campaign Budget Optimization)
  Success metric: Cost per result within 2x of target

Phase 2: OPTIMIZATION (Weeks 3-6) — 50% of budget
  Objective: Scale winners, kill losers, refine audiences
  Approach: Shift budget to top performers, narrow targeting, A/B test
  Success metric: Cost per result within 1.2x of target

Phase 3: SCALE (Weeks 7+) — 30% of budget
  Objective: Maximize volume at target efficiency
  Approach: Increase budgets 20% per week on winners, expand geo/audience
  Success metric: Maintain CPA while increasing volume 2-3x
```

**Monthly budget pacing template:**

| Week | % of Monthly Budget | Focus | Adjustments |
|------|-----------------------|-------|-------------|
| 1 | 20% | Launch all creatives, broad targeting | None — collect data |
| 2 | 22% | Pause bottom 30% of creatives | Shift budget to top performers |
| 3 | 28% | Scale top 3 ad sets by 20% | Introduce new creative variants |
| 4 | 30% | Maximum scale on proven winners | Prepare next month's creative |

### 5. Reach & Frequency Estimation

**Reach calculation:**

```
Estimated Reach = (Budget × 1,000) / CPM / Frequency

Example:
  Budget: $10,000
  CPM: $8.00
  Target Frequency: 3x
  Reach = ($10,000 × 1,000) / $8.00 / 3 = 416,667 unique users
```

**Optimal frequency by objective:**

| Objective | Optimal Frequency | Platform Cap | Over-Frequency Signal |
|-----------|-------------------|--------------|----------------------|
| Awareness | 2-4x/week | 3-5x/week | CTR drops below 0.3% |
| Consideration | 3-6x/week | 5-7x/week | Negative feedback rises |
| Conversion | 5-10x/week | 8-12x/week | CPA increases 20%+ |
| Retargeting | 3-5x/week | 5-7x/week | Hide/block rate above 1% |
